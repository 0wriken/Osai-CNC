日常维护说明|Daily Maintenance Instruction
1、每次工作前后对设备吹灰，清理各轴导轨及齿条灰尘|1. Blow dust off the equipment before and after each work, and clean the dust on the guide rails and racks.
2、每 3 天摇动摇杆 3 至 5 次对 Y/Z 轴进行润滑|2. Shake the rocker 3 to 5 times every 3 days and lubricate Y / Z axis.
3、每天对设备气源处理器的空气过滤器进行排水|3. Drain the air filter of air source processor daily.
4、设备自带的热机程序每天开机至少运行一次|4. Run the built-in heating program at least once a day when machine is started.
5、每周一次对储气罐进行排水|5. Drain the gas tank once a week.
6、每5-7天定期做一次钻轴清洁|6. Do a regular cleaning of drill shaft every 5-7 days.
7、每周对未连接油管的滑块用手动黄油枪添加0#机械润滑脂|7. Add 0 # mechanical grease to the slider unconnected to oil pipe with a manual grease gun every week.
8、气排钻每80-120个工作小时注入润滑脂，润滑脂型号(KLUBER ISOFLEX TOPAS L32N)请勿注入其它型号润滑脂|8. Fill the phneumatic drill head with grease every 80-120 working hours. Grease model (KLUBER ISOFLEX TOPAS L32N) Do not inject other models of grease.
9、真空泵每工作500小时后在运转时向滚动轴承的两处油嘴上注入高温润滑油脂15克（KLUBER）|9. After every 500 hours of operation of the vacuum pump, 15 grams of high-temperature lubricating grease (KLUBER) needs to be injected into the two nipples of the rolling bearing during operation.
10、禁止客户私自拆改机器, 因私自拆改或未使用原厂配件造成机器的损坏由客户自行承担责任|10. It is forbidden for the customer to dismantle and modify the machine without permission. The customer shall bear the responsibility for the damage caused by the unauthorized disassembly or the disuse of original accessories.
警告:一切维护保养工作须在断电的状态下进行!|Warning: all maintenance work must be carried out when power off !
